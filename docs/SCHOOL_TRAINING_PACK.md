## Recommended Rollout Sequence

1. Share the School SOP with all departments.
2. Train operational staff with the staff quick reference.
3. Train admins/managers with the manager quick reference.
4. Run practical walkthrough:
   - Student registration/enrollment
   - Fee posting and receipt issuance
   - Attendance entry
   - Exception approval flow
   - Daily reconciliation

## Training Completion Checklist

- Staff can process student records correctly.
- Staff can post payments and issue receipts accurately.
- Staff understand when approval is required.
- Managers can perform reconciliation and exception review.
- Teams understand end-of-day closure controls.
