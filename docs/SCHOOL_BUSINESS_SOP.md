# School Business SOP (One-Page Guide)

This guide defines standard daily operating procedures for organizations using the School business type.

## 1) Start of Day

- Sign in with your own staff account (no shared logins).
- Confirm organization and term/period settings are correct.
- Check dashboard alerts for pending actions:
  - Fee balances
  - Attendance gaps
  - Unposted transactions
  - Approval items

## 2) Core Master Data Controls

- Keep core records current:
  - Students
  - Guardians/contacts
  - Classes/streams
  - Staff
  - Fee structures
- Do not duplicate student profiles; search before creating.
- Any structural changes (fees/classes) must be approved by school admin/finance lead.

## 3) Student Admission and Enrollment

- Register learner with complete profile and guardian contacts.
- Assign class/stream and effective date.
- Confirm billing setup (fee plan, discounts/scholarship, opening balance if applicable).
- Print or share enrollment confirmation where required.

## 4) Fees and Billing Operations

- Generate charges according to approved fee structure and term.
- Capture payments with correct method and receipt reference.
- Issue receipts immediately after confirmed payment.
- Use approved workflow for:
  - Discounts
  - Waivers
  - Reversals/refunds
- Reconcile fee postings with payment totals daily.

## 5) Attendance and Academic Operations

- Record attendance by class on schedule (morning/afternoon as configured).
- Resolve missing or conflicting attendance entries same day.
- Enter marks/assessments only in assigned subjects/classes.
- Review report outputs before publishing.

## 6) Expenses and Procurement

- Record school expenses with proper category and supporting note/reference.
- Use approved vendor workflow and payment controls.
- Ensure purchases/stock usage (if enabled) are posted to the correct accounts.

## 7) Reporting and Reconciliation

- Daily checks:
  - Collections by method
  - Outstanding balances
  - Cash/bank position
- Period checks:
  - Fee aging
  - Class attendance summaries
  - Income/expense vs budget
- Escalate anomalies immediately (missing receipts, unmatched totals, duplicate records).

## 8) Security and Approval Rules

- Role-based access must be enforced (teacher, bursar, admin, manager).
- Sensitive actions require approval and reason:
  - Fee adjustment
  - Refund/void
  - Back-dated edits
- Never share manager PIN/password credentials.

## 9) End of Day Checklist

- All today’s payments posted and receipted.
- Pending approvals reviewed.
- Attendance completion checked.
- Cash/card/mobile summaries reconciled to system totals.
- Exceptions logged for next-day follow-up.

## 10) Do/Don’t Rules

### Do

- Verify learner identity before posting financial changes.
- Use comments/notes for all exceptional transactions.
- Follow approval chains for overrides.

### Don’t

- Do not edit or delete financial records without authorization.
- Do not use another user’s account.
- Do not close day with unreconciled collections.
